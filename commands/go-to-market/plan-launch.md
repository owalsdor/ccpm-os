# /plan-launch -- Go-to-Market Strategy

Orchestrates a complete GTM plan by chaining three skills in sequence: beachhead → ICP → GTM strategy.

## Invocation

```
/plan-launch AI-powered proposal writer for consulting firms
/plan-launch New enterprise tier for our project management tool
/plan-launch [upload a PRD, strategy doc, or pitch deck]
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand the Launch

Ask a few framing questions: what's being launched, stage (pre/imminent/post), existing customers or zero, timeline, budget, team size. Accept uploaded context.

### Step 2: Chain the Skills

Apply the following skills in order, passing context forward between them:

1. **beachhead-segment** — identify the single best starting segment.
2. **ideal-customer-profile** — define the ICP within that beachhead.
3. **gtm-strategy** — build positioning, messaging, channels, tactics, and success metrics for that ICP.

Let each skill drive its own output format and templates. At the end, stitch the three outputs into a single GTM plan document.

### Step 3: Offer Next Steps

- "Want me to **design growth loops** for post-launch traction?" (chains to growth-loops + gtm-motions)
- "Should I **create competitive battlecards** for sales?"
- "Want me to **build sales enablement materials**?"
- "Should I **build a metrics dashboard** for launch tracking?"

## Notes

- "Everyone" is not a segment — the tighter the beachhead, the faster you learn
- The ICP should be specific enough that sales/marketing can identify prospects in 30 seconds
- Pre-launch activities (waitlist, beta, early access) are as important as launch day
- Plan for post-launch: the first 90 days determine long-term trajectory
