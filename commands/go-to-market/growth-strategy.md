# /growth-strategy -- Growth Loops & GTM Motions

Orchestrates a growth plan by chaining two skills: `growth-loops` for sustainable compounding mechanisms, and `gtm-motions` for the acquisition channel mix.

## Invocation

```
/growth-strategy B2B collaboration tool — growth has stalled at 5K users
/growth-strategy Consumer fitness app looking for viral growth
/growth-strategy [upload product metrics or growth data]
```

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand Growth Context

Ask: what the product is, current metrics and growth rate, what's working/not, business model, and available budget/team. Accept uploaded data.

### Step 2: Chain the Skills

1. **growth-loops** — evaluate the 5 loop types (viral, usage, collaboration, UGC, referral) and recommend a primary + secondary loop.
2. **gtm-motions** — assess the 7 motions (inbound, outbound, paid, community, partners, ABM, PLG) and recommend the motion mix.

Let each skill produce its own output. Then synthesize into a combined growth strategy with a 90-day plan.

### Step 3: Offer Next Steps

- "Want me to **plan a specific launch campaign**?" (chains to plan-launch)
- "Should I **set up metrics** to track growth loop health?"
- "Want me to **design a referral program**?"
- "Should I **draft marketing ideas** for the inbound motion?"

## Notes

- Growth loops compound; tactics don't — prioritize loops over one-off campaigns
- The best growth loop uses the product itself as the channel (PLG, viral, collaboration)
- CAC should be < 1/3 of LTV for sustainable growth — flag if projected CAC is too high
- Budget follows learning: invest small in experiments, then scale what proves out
